About us

Our Brief History and Core Values

Brief history

We trace our beginnings from year 2009 as a collaboration of technology of Technology Professionals joined together by a shared mission of helping small offices both public and privately owned, who does not have the capability to evaluate and organize their internal IT department to enable and operate their IT requirements and resources.

For the past years the group is successfuly serving offices of diverse nature such as Contact Centers, Law Firms, Accounting Firms, Freight Forwarding Business and Government Offices and aquired different international Standard and Technology Certification and Expertise such as ISO, CMMI, ITIL, Six Sigma, Cisco, Microsoft, Linux and otherswhich enables us to passionately serve with excellence.

Core Values

Our customers are the Reason of Our Existence, Our Employees and Partners Keeps Us Moving and Our Leader Inspire Us on the Right Direction, with Aligned Objective We can Yield the Best Output for Everyone.

Foundation

Is a profitable and Evolving Community of Problem Solvers and Technolgy Professionals Built in the Foundation of Honor, Cooperation, Friendship and Patriotism

Vision

To be a Significant Contributor to the Information and Communication Technology Industry and be Recognize as One of the Top Local ICT Provider in terms of Profitability and Quality of Service

Mission

To Contribute Radically on our Customers Success by Providing Technical Services with embedded Excellence and Develop Industry Experts.

services

IT Service and Quality Management Consulting

From Capability Assessment to Root Cause Analysis to help customer improve their Business Efficiency and Capacity using International Standard and Best Practices Methods like ITIL, ISO, Lean Six Sigma and others.

IT Technical Support

An end to end service provider typically for SMEs covering all their IT needs from helping the customer decide which computer(s) to buy and where to buy them, building their office network, to providing support for internet connection problems and desktop concerns such as application installation, files backup, windows OS troubleshooting and others.

System and Network Design, Development, Implementation, Administration and Optimization

Custom fit systems/application software development according to customers requirements from complex enterprise portal to simple web page design. Designing customers IT infrastructure to support their business needs, implementing customers own design for their internal use for their client requirements, Integrating new system or network with their existing infrastructure, monitoring and improving customers systems and networks availability.

Personal Computer and Server Administration

Day to day Desktop Support and Server Administration typically for large enterprises, from installation of applications, troubleshooting hardware and software, to monitoring Server Application availability

Service, Software and CPE Quality Assurance Testing

An external Quality Assurance Service for Businesses that are looking for third party to test their own product/services before commercial deployment or a third party to test their acquired services or product from their supplier or vendor
